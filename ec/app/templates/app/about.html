About Patil Dairy

Our commitment to delivering the finest dairy products to your doorstep with utmost care and quality.

Our History

Patil Dairy was established in 1995 with a vision to bring fresh, organic dairy products to homes across the region. Over the years, we've grown from a small local dairy farm to a trusted name in the industry, serving thousands of satisfied customers.

Our Mission

We aim to provide nutritious, fresh, and affordable dairy products while ensuring the highest standards of animal welfare and environmental sustainability.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our products.
  • Customer-Centric: Our customers' satisfaction is our top priority.
  • Integrity: We are committed to ethical and transparent practices.
  • Sustainability: We believe in protecting the environment for future generations.
